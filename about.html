Saltimbocca

Located near Piazza Navona. This reasturant is one of my favorites for it's pasta. My two favorites are pasta al basilico or pasta amatriciana!

L'Antica Birreria Peroni

Located near the Trevi Fountain. This italian reasturant has a very authentic feel and you will rarely find tourists there. My favorite dish is their lasagna!

Enoteca Corsi

Located near Piazza Venezia. This reasturant has also a very authentic italian atmosphere. They have less items on their menu, but their pasta is amazing.

Pastaciutta

Located near the Vatican. They have really cheap and amazing pasta. My favorite thing was to take the pasta to go and eat it on the steps of the Vatican.
